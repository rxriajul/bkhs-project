Admission Process

At Baypail Kinder Garten and High School, we strive to make the admission process as smooth and straightforward as possible. Below are the steps to apply for admission:

  1. Complete the online application form.
  2. Submit required documents (birth certificate, previous school records, etc.).
  3. Attend an interview or assessment (if applicable).
  4. Receive admission decision via email or postal mail.
  5. Complete enrollment by paying the admission fee and tuition.

Tuition & Fees

Our tuition and fee structure for the academic year is as follows:

  • Kindergarten: $4,000 per year
  • Elementary School (Grades 1-5): $5,000 per year
  • Middle School (Grades 6-8): $6,000 per year
  • High School (Grades 9-12): $7,000 per year

Additional fees may apply for extracurricular activities, textbooks, and school uniforms.

Scholarships & Financial Aid

Baypail Kinder Garten and High School offers various scholarships and financial aid options to assist families in need. To apply for financial aid, please follow these steps:

  1. Complete the financial aid application form.
  2. Submit proof of income and other required documents.
  3. Attend an interview with the financial aid committee (if applicable).
  4. Receive the financial aid decision and complete the acceptance process.

For more information, please contact our admissions office at admissions@baypailkgandhs.com.
